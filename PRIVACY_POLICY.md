Privacy Policy for Galerinio
Effective Date: April 28, 2026

Last Updated: April 28, 2026

Galerinio ("we", "our", or "us") is an open-source multimedia gallery application for Android. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled securely and transparently.

1. Information Collection and Use
a. User Accounts and Identity
Galerinio requests the android.permission.GET_ACCOUNTS permission. This is used exclusively to facilitate the Google Drive Synchronization feature. We do not collect, store, or share your email address or identity on any external servers managed by us.

b. Media Files and Storage
The app requires access to your device's storage to display and manage your photos and videos. Your media files remain on your device unless you manually choose to upload them to a third-party storage provider (Google Drive, SMB, SFTP, or WebDAV).

c. Location Data (EXIF)
The "Map View" feature processes EXIF metadata stored within your images locally on your device to display them on a map. This data is not transmitted to us.

2. Third-Party Services
Galerinio integrates with the following third-party services:

Google Drive API: Used for backing up and syncing media. Data handling is subject to the Google Privacy Policy.

Google Maps SDK: Used to provide the Geo-tagging feature.

Custom Protocols (SMB/SFTP/WebDAV): Data transferred via these protocols is sent directly to the server addresses provided by you. We have no access to these servers or the data transferred.

3. Data Encryption and Security
We implement industry-standard security measures. When syncing with Google Drive or other cloud providers, data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/SSL or SSH).

4. Open Source Transparency
Galerinio is based on the Fossify Gallery and is licensed under GPLv3. Our source code is public and available for audit at: (https://github.com/deminio55/Galerinio). This ensures that there are no hidden data collection mechanisms.

5. Data Retention and Deletion
We do not store your data on our own servers. Any data synced to cloud providers (like Google Drive) is managed by you and can be deleted through those respective services.

6. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us via GitHub by opening an issue in our repository.
